Straight answers

Before you put a seller's name on it.

The pilot is intentionally transparent about what is automated and what is manually reviewed.

Is this already a fully automated AI platform?

No. This market-test MVP sells a concierge pilot. AI assists production and human review protects property facts, brand consistency and output quality. Repeated workflows will be automated after paid demand is proven.

Will you invent furniture or property details?

Staging concepts are clearly identified and never used to alter structural facts, dimensions, condition or included features. The agent approves every transformed asset before publication.

Can we use our own logo, fonts and agent voice?

Yes. Team and Brokerage pilots include a reusable brand profile. Voice cloning is used only with explicit, recorded consent from the person whose voice is replicated.

What do you need from us?

Approved photos, verified property facts, brand assets, target channels and confirmation that you have permission to use the submitted material.
